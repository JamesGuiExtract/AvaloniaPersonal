    • [FlexIDSCore #4105, #4166, #4541, #4562, #4565, #4725] Redaction resizing
      • Verify some documents that include both angular and rectangular redactions.
      • Click the grip handle of a redaction
        • Confirm that slightly moving the mouse cursor does not cause the handle's side of the redaction to jump to the mouse cursor.
      • Click the grip handle of a very small (1x1) redaction
        • Confirm that the redaction does not change size.
        • Confirm that the redaction does not move.
      • Create a redaction at a significant angle
      • Use the shrink selection context menu option (possibly several times) to shrink all sides of the redaction
        • Confirm that the redaction continues to shrink.
      • Create a long redaction at a slight angle
      • Use the + shortcut several times to expand the redaction
        • Confirm that the angle of the redaction remains unchanged.
      • Experiment with shrinking and enlarging angled redactions
        • Confirm that the angle of the redaction remains unchanged.
      • Select a highlight
        • Press and hold the + and - shortcuts to repeatedly grow and shrink the highlights by large amounts.
        • Confirm that highlight does not move its center position.
        • Confirm that when returned to its original size, the aspect ratio is unchanged.
