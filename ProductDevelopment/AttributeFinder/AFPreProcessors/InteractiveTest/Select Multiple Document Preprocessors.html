Select Multiple Document Preprocessors Dialog

  • Confirm that the list box contains two columns: Enabled and Description.
  • Confirm that the newly added Preprocessor is Enabled by default.
  • Confirm that Enabled and Disabled settings are persistent in saved RSD files.
  • Confirm that enabled preprocessors are exercised during rule execution.
  • Confirm that disabled preprocessors are not exercised during rule execution.

  • Confirm that the Add button displays the Select Document Processor dialog (See Document Preprocessor Page).
  • Confirm that highlighting an existing clue in the list box entry activates the Delete and Configure buttons.
  • Confirm that CTRL + Left Click allows for multiple selection of list box items.
  • Confirm that SHIFT + Left Click allows for multiple selection of list box items.
  • Confirm that Right Click on the list box displays a context menu.

    Context Menu Options

    1. Confirm that Cut exists.
      1. Confirm that items can be successfully cut to the clipboard.
      2. Confirm that clicking on Cut displays a Confirm Delete dialog.
      3. Confirm that Cut successfully cuts multiple selected items.
    2. Confirm that Copy exists.
      1. Confirm that items can be successfully copied to the clipboard.
      2. Confirm that Copy successfully copies multiple selected items.
    3. Confirm that Paste exists.
      1. Confirm that items can be successfully pasted from the clipboard.
      2. Confirm that Paste successfully pastes multiple cut or copied items.
    4. Confirm that Delete exists.
      1. Confirm that the item is deleted successfully.
      2. Confirm that clicking on Delete displays a Confirm Delete dialog.
      3. Confirm that Delete successfully deletes multiple selected items.
    5. Confirm that Cancel exists.
      1. Confirm that Cancel exits the context menu successfully.
