LabDE 1.5.0.101 Testing

Purpose and Details

  • Purpose: Exercise recent fixed SCRs
  • Details: Recently added SCRs generally do not have official test cases present within the collected checklists.

SCRs

  1. [DataEntry #149, #752] Docking windows
    • Confirm that the DEP window and the image window can be docked separately and displayed on separate monitors.

  2. [DataEntry #720, #722, #764] LabDE installation
    • Review text on the Install - Other Products page in the DemoShield installation.
    • Confirm that inappropriate apostrophes are not present.
    • Confirm that text is not obscured.
    • Confirm that the correct fax number is provided in the license agreement.
    • Confirm that the correct Transfer may not be made to an unrelated company or to a related company text is provided in the license agreement.

  3. [DataEntry #749] Smart tags
    • Confirm that smart tags are supported correctly - see Steve for more information.

  4. [FlexIDSCore #3856] Apostrophes in comment field
    • Confirm that apostrophes in the Comments edit box no longer double after revisitation.

  5. [LegacyRC #5568, #5605] New database tables
    • Confirm that a FAMSession table exists in the database.
    • Confirm that a SaveFAMSessionHistory row exists in the DBInfo table.
    • Confirm that the SaveFAMSessionHistory default is 1.
    • Confirm that FAMSession records with start and stop times are added for each FAM instance.
    • Confirm that FAMSession records are not added for SaveFAMSessionHistory = 0.

    • Confirm that an InputEvent table exists in the database.
    • Confirm that an EnableInputEventTracking row exists in the DBInfo table.
    • Confirm that an InputEventHistorySize row exists in the DBInfo table.
    • Confirm that the EnableInputEventTracking default is 1.
    • Confirm that the InputEventHistorySize default is 30.
