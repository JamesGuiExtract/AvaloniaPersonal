'Pan', 'zoom window', 'zoom in', 'zoom out, 'zoom next' and 'zoom previous' tools.

  1. Click on the 'New Spot Recognition Window' button.

    IcoMap opens a new 'Spot Recognition' dialog and brings up the 'Open' dialog box right away.

  2. Open the image ImageRecognitionImage6.bmp.

    IcoMap opens the specified file.

  3. Zoom window to 'A'.

  4. Pan to 'Q'. It should take 2 pan movements.

  5. Zoom in 3 times.

  6. Pan to 'K'. It should take 7 pan movements.

  7. Zoom Extents.

  8. Zoom window to 'W'.

  9. Pan to 'T'. It should take 10 pan movements.

  10. Zoom out 8 times.

  11. Pan to 'H'. It should take 3 pan movements.

  12. Zoom previous 3 times.

    You should be back to 'H'.

  13. Zoom Extents.

  14. Zoom window to 'Z'.

  15. Pan to 'V'. It should take 4 pan movements.

  16. Zoom out 7 times.

  17. Pan to 'F'. It should take 2 pan movements.

  18. Zoom in 3 times.

  19. Zoom Extents.

  20. Zoom window to 'M'.

  21. Using the slider bar, pan to the left until you reach 'N'.

  22. Zoom previous 1 time.

    You should be back to zoom extents.

  23. Zoom window to 'J'.

  24. Using the slider bar, pan to the right until you reach 'O'.

  25. Using the slider bar, pan up until you reach 'D'.

  26. Zoom out 3 times.

  27. Pan to 'Y'. It should take 3 pan movements.

  28. Zoom window to 'Y'.

  29. Pan to 'B'. It should take 4 pan movements.

  30. Zoom out 5 times.

  31. Zoom Extents.

  32. Zoom window to 'E'.

  33. Using the slider bar, pan to down until you reach 'P'.

  34. Pan to 'U'. It should take 3 pan movements.

  35. Pan to 'X'. It should take 2 pan movements.

  36. Zoom in 5 times.

  37. Zoom Extents.

  38. Zoom window to 'R'.

  39. Zoom Extents.

  40. Zoom window to 'I'.

  41. Zoom Extents.

  42. Zoom window to 'C'.

  43. Zoom previous 2 times.

    You should be back to 'I'.

  44. Zoom previous 2 times.

    You should be back to 'R'.

  45. Zoom previous 2 times.

    You should be back to 'X'.

  46. Zoom previous 7 times.

    You should be back to 'U'.

  47. Zoom previous 5 times.

    You should be back to 'B'. You should have gone past 'E'.

  48. Zoom previous 13 times.

    You should be back to 'D'. You should have gone past 'Y'.

  49. Zoom previous 10 times.

    You should be back to 'V'. You should have gone past 'O', 'J' and 'F'.

  50. Zoom previous 31 times.

    You should be back to 'W'. You should have gone past 'Z', 'H', and 'T'.

  51. Zoom previous 14 times.

    You should be back to 'A'. You should have gone past 'K', and 'Q'.

  52. Zoom previous 1 time.

    The zoom previous button should be disabled.

  53. Click the zoom next tool until it becomes disabled.

    It should have stopped on 'C'.
