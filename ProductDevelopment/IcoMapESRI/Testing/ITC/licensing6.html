IcoMap - Advanced Concurrent Licensing

  1. Prepare two separate IcoMap test machines (A and B). Each machine should be configured for a concurrent license.

    Confirm that the USB key contains exactly one IcoMap license.

  2. Start ArcMap on A.

    Confirm that IcoMap starts without error. Confirm that the Extensions dialog (from Tools menu) includes Extract Systems IcoMap and that the entry is checked. Close ArcMap on A.

  3. Start ArcMap on B.

    Confirm that IcoMap starts without error. Confirm that the Extensions dialog (from Tools menu) includes Extract Systems IcoMap and that the entry is checked. Do not close ArcMap on B.

  4. Remove the USB key.

    Confirm that IcoMap can no longer be used and that a meaningful error is provided. Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog is unchecked. Close ArcMap on B.

  5. Replace the USB key. Start ArcMap on B. Do not start IcoMap.

    Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog is unchecked. Do not close ArcMap on B.

  6. Start IcoMap on B from the IcoMap toolbar button.

    Confirm that IcoMap starts without error. Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog is checked. Do not close ArcMap on B.

  7. [P10 #3107] Start ArcMap on A.

    Confirm that ArcMap starts without error and that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog is unchecked. Do not close ArcMap on A.

  8. Attempt to check the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog on A.

    Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog remains unchecked. Confirm that a meaningful error message is provided. Do not close ArcMap on A.

    [P10: #3111] Confirm that the ArcMap Arrow tool is selected.

  9. Uncheck the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog on B.

    Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog on A can be checked. Close ArcMap on A. Do not close ArcMap on B.

  10. Start ArcMap on A.

    Confirm that the Extract Systems IcoMap entry in the Extensions dialog is checked. Confirm that IcoMap cannot be started on B.

  11. Close ArcMap on A. Close ArcMap on B.
