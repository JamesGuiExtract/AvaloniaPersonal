Party Finder

  • The Party Finder captures, trims, and splits parties on land record documents.
  • The party finder rules can be called from <ComponentDataDir>\Indexing\PartyFinder\Master.rsd.etf
  • The best practice for using the party finder is to use a Document Type Condition to select the document type you want to work with, and then call the attribute of the party you want to find.
    • For example, if you wanted to find mortgagors on mortgage documents, you would create a Conditionally Find Value value finder, give it a Document Type Condition that will pass if the document type is Mortgage, and then call the Mortgagor attribute from <ComponentDataDir>\Indexing\PartyFinder\Master.rsd.etf .
  • The most useful parties that are available are:
    • Mortgage
      • Mortgagor - The party that is borrowing money.
      • Mortgagor_backup - Backup rules for the Mortgagor party.
      • Mortgagee - The party that is lending money.
      • Mortgagee_backup - Backup rules for the Mortgagee party.

    • Deed of Trust
      • Beneficiary - The party that is lending money.
      • Trustee - The party that serving as trustee. This party holds the title to the property.
      • Trustor - The party that is borrowing money.
      • Trustor_backup - Backup rules for the Trustor party.

    • Assignment of Mortgage
      • Assignee - The party that is receiving the rights to the loan.
      • Assignee_backup - Backup rules for the Assignee party.
      • Assignor - The party that is giving up the rights to the loan.
      • Assignor_backup - Backup rules for the Assignor party.
      • OriginalMortgagee - The lender that originally made the loan.
      • OriginalMortgagor - The borrower that originally made the loan.

    • Assignment of Deed of Trust
      • Assignee - The party that is receiving the rights to the loan.
      • Assignee_backup - Backup rules for the Assignee party.
      • Assignor - The party that is giving up the rights to the loan.
      • Assignor_backup - Backup rules for the Assignor party.
      • OriginalBeneficiary - The lender that originally made the loan.
      • OriginalTrustee - The trustee of the original loan.
      • OriginalTrustor - The borrower that originally made the loan.

    • Reconveyance
      • Beneficiary - The lender that is releasing the loan.
      • OriginalBeneficiary - The lender that originally made the loan.
      • OriginalTrustee - The trustee of the original loan.
      • OriginalTrustor - The borrower that originally made the loan.
      • Trustee - The party that is reconveying the property.
      • Trustee_backup - Backup rules for the Trustee party.

    • Satisfaction of Mortgage
      • Mortgagee - The lender that is releasing the loan.
      • Mortgagee_backup - Backup rules for the Mortgagee party.
      • OriginalMortgagee - The lender that originally made the loan.
      • OriginalMortgagor - The borrower that originally made the loan.

    • Substitution of Trustee
      • Beneficiary - The lender that currently holds the rights to the loan.
      • Beneficiary_backup - Backup rules for the Beneficiary party.
      • OriginalBeneficiary - The lender that originally made the loan.
      • OriginalTrustee - The trustee that is being replaced.
      • OriginalTrustor - The borrower that originally made the loan.
      • Trustee - The trustee that is being substituted by the Beneficiary.
      • Trustee_backup - Backup rules for the Trustee party.

    • Warranty Deed
      • Grantee - The party that is obtaining the rights to the property.
      • Grantor - The party giving up rights to the property.
      • Grantor_backup - Backup rules for the Grantor field.

    • Quit Claim Deed
      • Grantee - The party that is obtaining the rights to the property.
      • Grantor - The party giving up rights to the property.
      • Grantor_backup - Backup rules for the Grantor field.
