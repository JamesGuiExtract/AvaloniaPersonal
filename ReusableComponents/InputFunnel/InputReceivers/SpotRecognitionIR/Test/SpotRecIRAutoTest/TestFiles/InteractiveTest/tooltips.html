Tooltips, status bar and coordinates.

  1. Open the file IMAGENAME???.


    NOTE: If you need more information on opening images go to either of the following test cases:
    Opening_files.html

  2. Check to see if there are reasonable tooltips for each of the toolbar buttons.

  3. Click the 'Zoom window' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Zoom window'.

  4. Click the 'Pan' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Pan'.

  5. Click the 'Highlighter' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Select text'.

  6. Click the 'Highlight height' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Set highlight height'.

  7. Click the 'Edit highlight text' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Select highlight to edit text'.

  8. Click the 'Delete highlights' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Select highlights to delete'.

  9. Click the 'Recognize text in window' button.

    The status bar at the bottom left should display 'Select window to recognize text in'.

  10. Move the cursor around within the bounds of the image.

    You should see the X, Y coordinate values change. Image X, Y places the origin (0,0) in the upper left hand corner of the image.
