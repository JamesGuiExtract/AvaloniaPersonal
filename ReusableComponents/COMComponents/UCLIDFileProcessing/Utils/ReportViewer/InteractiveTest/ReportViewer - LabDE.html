Report specific features

  1. Detailed usage report by specific user for specific day
    • Confirm that a VerifyUserName parameter is required.
    • For a particular user name and Range option:
      • Confirm that a meaningful report is presented.
      • Create a saved report and confirm that the associated XML file contains a DateRangeParameter node with Name = "ReportingPeriod" and Default = "<Range>".
      • Confirm that the associated XML file also contains a TextParameter node with Name = "VerifyUserName" and Default = "<name>".

    • Test with meaningful data
      • Confirm that the User and date table and contents are formatted properly even at 400% zoom factor.
      • Confirm that the User name information is correct.
      • Confirm that the Date information is correct.

      • Confirm that the Hours and minutes table and contents are formatted properly even at 400% zoom factor.
      • Confirm that the table title is displayed in white text on a black background.
      • Confirm that the top two rows of the table represent minutes within the hour ranging (vertically) between 0 and 59.
      • Confirm that the leftmost column displays specific hours starting with 6:00 AM and ending with 6:00 PM.
      • Confirm that some remaining cells in the table have a white background to indicate no input event activity during the specific minute.
      • Confirm that other remaining cells in the table have a colored background to indicate input event activity during the specific minute.

      • Confirm that after the Hours and minutes table is an entry for Total minutes with input event activity.
      • Confirm that the total is correct based on the shaded cells in the table.

    • Test with empty database
      • Confirm that the User and date table and contents are formatted properly even at 400% zoom factor.
      • Confirm that the User name information is N/A.
      • Confirm that the Date information is correct.

      • Confirm that the Hours and minutes table and contents are formatted properly even at 400% zoom factor.
      • Confirm that the table title is displayed in white text on a black background.
      • Confirm that the top two rows of the table represent minutes within the hour ranging (vertically) between 0 and 59.
      • Confirm that the leftmost column displays specific hours starting with 6:00 AM and ending with 6:00 PM.
      • Confirm that the remaining cells in the table have a white background to indicate no input event activity during the specific minute.

      • Confirm that after the Hours and minutes table is an entry for Total minutes with input event activity.
      • Confirm that the total is 0.
