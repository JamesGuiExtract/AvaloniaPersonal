Image Viewer Zoom and Rotation Testing Checklist

    Zoom - Manual Tests

    • Zoom window - from Tools menu
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Zoom in several steps.
      • Click the Pan toolbar button.
      • Select Zoom window from the Tools menu.
      • Click and drag the mouse in the Image Viewer.
      • Confirm that a box is drawn describing the zoom area.
      • Release the mouse button and confirm that the image zooms to the selected region.

    • Zoom window - from Toolbar
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Zoom in several steps.
      • Click the Pan toolbar button.
      • Click the Zoom window toolbar button.
      • Click and drag the mouse in the Image Viewer.
      • Confirm that a box is drawn describing the zoom area.
      • Release the mouse button and confirm that the image zooms to the selected region.

    • [DotNet #31] Zoom sequence is effective
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Click the Fit to page toolbar button.
      • Zoom out one step.
      • Zoom previous.
      • Zoom next.
      • Confirm that the entire image is visible in the Image Viewer and that it remains zoomed out one step.

    Rotation - Manual Tests

    • [DotNet #32] Center point is unchanged after rotation
      • Open an image and zoom in so that the center point of the display is obvious.
      • Rotate the image.
      • Confirm that the center point is unchanged after rotation.

    • [DotNet #35] Angular highlights remain after rotation
      • Open an image and zoom in so that the center point of the display is obvious.
      • Create an angular highlight.
      • Rotate the image.
      • Confirm that the highlight was rotated properly.

    • [DotNet #66] Annotated image rotates properly even if annotations are not licensed
      • Create a license file where annotations are not licensed.
      • Confirm that no other license files are available.
      • Open an image that contains one or more annotations.
      • Confirm that the annotations are not displayed.
      • Rotate the image.
      • Confirm that the image rotates properly and that annotations are still not displayed.
