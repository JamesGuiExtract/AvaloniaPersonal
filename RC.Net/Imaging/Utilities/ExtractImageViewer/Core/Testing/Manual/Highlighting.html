Image Viewer Highlighting Testing Checklist

    Angled Highlights - Manual Tests

    • Highlight Height - from toolbar
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Zoom in several steps.
      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and begin creating an angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the + key on the keyboard.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the + key on the number pad.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.

      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the - key on the keyboard.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the - key on the number pad.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.

      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, scroll the mouse wheel up.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.

      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, scroll the mouse wheel down.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.

    • Highlight Height - from Tools menu
      • Click the Tools - Highlighter menu item and begin creating an angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the + key on the keyboard.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the + key on the number pad.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.

      • Click the Tools - Highlighter menu item and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the - key on the keyboard.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, click the - key on the number pad.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.

      • Click the Tools - Highlighter menu item and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, scroll the mouse wheel up.
      • Confirm that the highlight height increases.

      • Click the Tools - Highlighter menu item and begin creating another angular highlight.
      • While continuing to hold down the mouse button, scroll the mouse wheel down.
      • Confirm that the highlight height decreases.
      • [DotNet #82] Confirm that the highlight height does not decrease to zero.

    Highlight Selection - Manual Tests

    • Shift + Selection - from toolbar
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and create several highlights.
      • Zoom in several steps.
      • Click the Select highlight toolbar button and select a highlight.
      • [DotNet #79] Confirm that the selected highlight has a dashed line border.
      • Press the Shift key and click one or more additional highlights.
      • Confirm that each highlight remains selected with gripper corners.

    • Control + Selection - from toolbar
      • Deselect all of the previous highlights.
      • Click the Select highlight toolbar button and select one highlight.
      • Press the Control key and click one or more additional highlights.
      • Confirm that each highlight remains selected with gripper corners.
      • Continue pressing the Control key and click one of the already selected highlights.
      • Confirm that the highlight is deselected.

    • Shift + Selection - from Tools menu
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and create several highlights.
      • Zoom in several steps.
      • Click the Tools - Select highlight menu item and select a highlight.
      • Press the Shift key and click one or more additional highlights.
      • Confirm that each highlight remains selected with gripper corners.

    • Control + Selection - from Tools menu
      • Deselect all of the previous highlights.
      • Click the Tools - Select highlight menu item and select one highlight.
      • [DotNet #83] Press the Control key and click one or more additional highlights.
      • Confirm that each highlight remains selected with gripper corners.
      • Continue pressing the Control key and click one of the already selected highlights.
      • Confirm that the highlight is deselected.

      • Draw an angled highlight and select it.
      • [DotNet #84] Put the mouse cursor over one of the grip handles and press the Control key without moving the mouse.
      • Confirm that the mouse cursor changes to the cross cursor indicating that the angle can be changed.

    • Click and Drag - from Tools menu
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and create several highlights.
      • Activate highlight selection by clicking Tools - Select highlight.
      • Confirm that clicking and dragging the mouse across several highlights works to select those highlights.

      • Press the Shift key and then click and drag across different highlights.
      • Confirm that the new highlights are added to the existing selection.

    • Click and Drag - from Toolbar
      • Open an image from the Redaction Demo.
      • Click the Highlighter toolbar button and create several highlights.
      • Activate highlight selection by clicking the Select layer object toolbar button.
      • Confirm that clicking and dragging the mouse across several highlights works to select those highlights.

      • Press the Shift key and then click and drag across different highlights.
      • Confirm that the new highlights are added to the existing selection.

      • Click the Select highlight toolbar button and select a rectangular highlight.

      • Click the Select highlight toolbar button and select a rectangular highlight.

      • [DotNet #100] Confirm that a right-click during a highlight resize operation has no effect.

      • Click the Select highlight toolbar button and select a rectangular highlight.
      • Confirm that the highlight cannot be dragged completely out of the image area.

      • [DotNet #99] Confirm that trying to move the highlight completely out of the image area results in the highlight retaining its original location.
